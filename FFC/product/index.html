Adopt A Marine Mammal

Help give a rescued marine mammal a chance of a new life by donating through adopting a mammal. While you won't own the rescued marine mammal, the money will help us care for them 24/7. We release all releasable marine mammals in our care once they are rehabilitated, others are given a safe home.

The Amazing Sea Otter
